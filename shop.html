Sales channel plan

Use the website as home base. Use seller platforms as cash registers.

This keeps Ramble University looking like a real brand while letting Gumroad, Etsy, or Payhip handle payment, delivery, taxes, receipts, and downloads.

Gumroad

Fastest for simple digital products, easy checkout links, and email updates to buyers. Good for testing offers quickly.

Etsy

Best marketplace discovery for printable PDFs, puzzle books, journals, and digital workbooks. Needs strong mockups and keywords.

Payhip

Clean digital product storefront with EU/UK VAT handling features and simple product pages. Good alternate checkout.
